National flag

            
1-The national flag of India is called the "Tiranga" or "Tricolor."
2-It has three horizontal stripes: saffron (top), white (middle), and green (bottom).
3-Saffron represents courage and sacrifice.
4-White stands for peace and truth.
5-Green symbolizes faith and prosperity.
6-The white stripe features a navy blue Ashoka Chakra with 24 spokes, representing the eternal wheel of law and dharma.
7-The flag was designed by Pingali Venkayya.
8-It was officially adopted on July 22, 1947.
9-The flag reflects India's independence, unity, and cultural diversity.
